# Xenari Example Phrases

Known-good examples for translator and LLM checks.

```text
ra mex ka neq ta zrent sa xa
I love you.
```

```text
ra neq ka mex ta zrent sa xa
You love me.
```

```text
ra mex ka neq ta zrent lo xa
I loved you.
```

```text
ra nu zrump ka req ha ta mrob ve xo
They'll build the door tomorrow. / bare English they defaults to plural known `req ha`.
```

```text
gral troz ra zra ka mex ta pyoquqab lo xo
thanks for solving that. / literally: thanks because you solved that.
```

```text
ra mex ka neq ta zrent sa xa ngu
I do not love you.
```

```text
ra mex ka neq ta zrent sa xo
I love you. / assumed or narration-default evidential.
```

```text
mex krengk frem
you little bitch. / direct vocative insult.
```

```text
ra fatyih ka vi qex ta zux vi sa xo
the alien is dangerous.
```

```text
ra vi qex ka neq ta toq sa xa
I see the alien.
```

```text
ra vi loco na nu qlon ka neq ta frig sa xo
I approach the figure by/near the lake.
```

```text
ra vi loco po brid ka vi cuq ta qruq vi sa xo
The wind blows the figure's hat away.
```

```text
ra neq po brid ka vi cuq ta qruq vi sa xo
The wind blows my hat away.
```
